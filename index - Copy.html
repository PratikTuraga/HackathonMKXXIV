About Us

Many local clinics across rural and suburban areas generally maintain patient records in physical form such as books and files. Many complications can arise such as loss or damage of patient records. Our product, CliniCare, is an easy and accessible method for doctors to store the patient data locally or upload it to the internet. With high reliability, easy accessibility and a simple to use UI, we aim to bring the convenience of technology to areas where it is so desperately needed and can practically be used by anyone. .

Key Features

  • Efficient patient data management
  • Secure access control
  • Automatic refill notifications
